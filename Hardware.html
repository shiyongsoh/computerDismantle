Upgrading your network card

If you are using the internet, the speed of your internet is determined by your internet service provider, However, if you have a small server or even a mesh of computer networks, transfering files between both of which can be determined by your network card.

What can you do?

For lapotops:

Flip over your laptop and procced to unscrew the screws. Open the cover with a bit of force. Flex of the cover will be observed.

Your network card is what looks like a rectangular circut board with two wire fitted on the board. Unplug the wires and unscrew.

The circut board shall then lift itself up. Gently take it out.

Replace the new board by inserting the new replacement at and angle.

Follow the instruction backwards to complete the installation

The drivers shall be installed automatically, or you can procced to device manager and install it manually.
